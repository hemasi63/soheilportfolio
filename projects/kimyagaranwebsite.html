Kimyagaran Pasargad Website

Role: UI/Ux Designer

Timeline: 1 months (Sep 2021)

Overview

Kimiagran Pasargad website is a simple website and the purpose of this site is easy access for customers around the world, especially in the Middle East, to product information and order online.

Context

Kimiagaran Pasargad is one of the leading Iranian manufacturer and distributor of chemical construction materials and products. Company's sales manager needed a modern website which design by last methods so that it can be easily seen on all devices, including laptops, tablets and mobile phones, and customers without any difficulty accessing to all product information and can also easily search for the product they want.

Competitive Analysis

The first step in my design process was to research similar sustainability website currently in the market. This will allow me to identify their strengths and opportunities for thier website. I did an analysis of five competitors. After reviewing competitors' websites, I had a meeting with the company's sales manager and listed the features that competing websites had. Finally, they needed a simple but beautifully designed and user-friendly website so that customers could easily access to product information.
