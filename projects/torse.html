Torse Website

Role: UI/Ux & Web Designer

Timeline: 4 months (March 2022- June 2022)

Overview

Torse is jewelry brand, combining hand-made jewelries with Persian traditional valuable art, woodwork and inlay.

Context

Brand owner had website wich is designed 5 years ago. He asked to redesign the website with less structural changes.Since the brand is afraid to make and sell handmade and luxury jewelry, the brand owner wanted the website to have a stylish and classic design.

Competitive Analysis

Like all projects, the first step in designing a website is to look at similar and competing websites. After reviewing similar websites and talking to the brand owner, I decided to use black and gold colors in the design. The prototype which I sent to the project owner was accepted. Although the structure of the site was not to be changed, I did make some minor changes to the product page, including product video, enquire form, social icons for shareing page and ...
